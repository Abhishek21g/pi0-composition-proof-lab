Prove remix vs compose before the robot demo.

π0.7's air fryer result wasn't a deploy checklist problem — it was an evaluation problem. PI manually searched the training manifest for tangential episodes. This lab automates that falsification: decompose a novel task, search episodes, issue a COMPOSE / REMIX / COACH_REQUIRED receipt.
